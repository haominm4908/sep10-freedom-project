Part A:Current tools

In mechanical engineering,or even engineering in general, tools are needed to build, fix, and sometimes break down things within their field. For example, the screwdriver is a tool needed to manually drill in a screw into 2 pieces of wood to connect to each other. Here are some examples:

  1. Screwdriver

    As mentioned before, Screwdrivers are used to help manually drill a Screw into a piece of material, and can be used to connect pieces.

  2. Screws,Nuts,n bolts

    Screws, Nuts, And bolts are all parts needed in Engineering. Screws are used to drill into pieces of material with the help of a screwdriver or a power drill with a screw attachment. Lug nuts, are a type of fastener used for Motor vehicles, and they can be found on ones tires. Bolts are another type of fastener, that work in conjointment with nuts.

  3. Drill

    A drill, or a power drill, is a electrical tool that produces a rapid circular motion. It comes with attachment that help with various situations. It can be used to drill a hole, combined with a screw, it can screw something in. It can help fasten a bolt as well.
